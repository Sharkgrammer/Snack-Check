Privacy Policy

Welcome to our app. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how we handle and protect your data.

Data Collection

Our app does not connect to the internet and does not collect any personal data from users. The app only stores some data locally on your device, specifically data about which days snacks can be purchased. This data is not shared, transmitted, or accessible to anyone other than the user.

Data Storage

The data about days is stored locally on your device. It is not uploaded to any servers or shared with third parties. You have full control over this data, and it can be deleted at any time by uninstalling the app or clearing its data.
